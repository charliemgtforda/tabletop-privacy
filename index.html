TableTop Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

TableTop (“the App”) is developed by Charlie Grant. Your privacy is important to us. This policy describes how the App handles information.

1. Information We Collect

TableTop does not collect, store, or share any personal information. All data such as team names, scores, and settings remain solely on your device.

2. Local Storage

The app uses local iOS storage (via @AppStorage) to save progress. This information never leaves your device or syncs online.

3. Third-Party Services

TableTop does not use analytics, ads, or tracking SDKs. It has no server connection and performs all logic locally on your device.

4. Data Retention

All data is deleted when you uninstall the app.

5. Children’s Privacy

TableTop does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13. It is safe for all ages.
